Xournal++ Mobile brings the power of Xournal++ note-taking to your iPad and iPhone.

Create, edit, and view .xopp files on the go. Import PDFs and annotate them with your Apple Pencil or finger. Full support for strokes, highlights, text, images, and LaTeX equations.

Features:
- Native .xopp file format support
- Apple Pencil support with palm rejection
- PDF import and annotation
- Pressure-sensitive pen, highlighter, and eraser tools
- Layer management
- Full undo/redo support
- iPad multi-tasking with Split View and Slide Over
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users
- Dark mode support
- Export and share pages as PNG

Xournal++ Mobile is an unofficial mobile port of the desktop Xournal++ application.
